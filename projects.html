Project 3: High-Performance Low Noise Amplifier (LNA)

üì∂ Ultra-Sensitive Signal Reception System

This project involves the design and implementation of a high-performance Low Noise Amplifier for advanced wireless communication systems. The LNA is specifically optimized to amplify weak signals while minimizing added noise, making it ideal for satellite communications, cellular networks, and radar systems. The design achieves excellent noise figure and gain characteristics across the required frequency band.

Key Features:

  • Ultra-low noise figure (< 0.5 dB)
  • High power gain (20+ dB)
  • Stable operation across temperature range
  • Input/output impedance matching
  • Frequency response optimization
  • PCB layout and manufacturing

Technologies Used:

HFSS RF Simulation ADS Circuit Design Low Noise FET PCB Design Multisim Measurement & Testing
